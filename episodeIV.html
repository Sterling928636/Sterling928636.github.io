Overview

A New Hope begins with Princess Leia’s capture and the Rebel mission to destroy the Death Star. With help from Obi-Wan Kenobi, Han Solo, and others, Luke helps deliver the final blow to the Empire’s new superweapon, the DeathStar.

Why it matters

  • Classic hero’s journey
  • Introduction of iconic characters
  • Innovative practical effects for its time
