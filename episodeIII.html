Overview

Revenge of the Sith takes us further into the future where war is all the galaxy knows. Anakin gets manipulated by Palpatine and turns to the dark side. All the clones forcibly turn on the Jedi, killing all but a few. Anakin, now Darth Vader, fights Obi Wan, his former master and loses. The now Emperor Palpatine, rescues Anakin, forever trapping him in a suit that makes him more robot than man. Padme, gives birth to two of Anakins children, Luke and Leia.

Key points

  1. Anakin’s turn
  2. Order 66 and Republic collapse
  3. Birth of Luke and Leia
