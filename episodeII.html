Overview

Attack of the Clones (Episode II) continues Anakin’s story 10 years after the previous movie. It shows the growing conflict in the galaxy. Hundreds of planets have decided to rebel against the Republic and engage in open war with droid armies. The film highlights the start of the Clone Wars and Anakin’s tragic choices.
