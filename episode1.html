Overview

The Phantom Menace (Episode I) introduces young Anakin Skywalker, Obi Wan Kenobi, and Padme Amidalla. Obi Wan and his master discover Anakin, a young child who unbeknownst to him, is super powerful with the force. They scheme a way to 'buy' Anakin from his master and convince the Jedi Council to allow him to be trained in the ways of the Jedi. Trade disputes occur between Naboo and the Trade federation, leading to an invasion with a droid army.
