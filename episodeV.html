Overview

In the Empire Strikes Back, the Rebel Alliance sets up base on Hoth, and after a dramatic battle, barely escape Darth Vader. Luke SKywalker goes to train with Jedi Grand Master Yoda, while Han Solo, Princess Leia, and Chewbacca are found by the empire in the Cloud City. Luke leaves Yoda to try and save his friends when he encounters Darth Vader, who reveals that he is his father.

Notable quotes
