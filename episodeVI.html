Overview

Return of the Jedi closes the original trilogy with the rescue of Han Solo by Luke and Leia, the final battle against the Empire on the forest moon Endor, destroying the shield Generator to the second Death Star with the help of local Ewoks, and the redemption of Darth Vader.
